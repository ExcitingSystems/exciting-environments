Pendulum
==================================================

Model Equation
--------------------------------------------------
.. math::

      u= l m g sin(\theta) + l^2 m \ddot{\theta}

Parameters
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
   
|:math:`l`:Length of the pendulum 
|:math:`m`:Mass of the pendulum tip 
|:math:`g`: Gravitational acceleration
