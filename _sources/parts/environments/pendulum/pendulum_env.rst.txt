Pendulum
==================================================

Model Equation
--------------------------------------------------
.. math::

      u= l m g sin(\theta) + l^2 m \ddot{\theta}

Parameters
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
   
\(l\):Length of the pendulum \\
\(m\):Mass of the pendulum tip \\
\(g\): Gravitational acceleration
