Mass Spring Damper

Description

Environment to simulate a Mass-Spring-Damper System.

Equation

\[F_t = m \ddot{y_t} + d \dot{y_t} + k y_t\]

Parameters

\(d\): Damping constant
\(k\): Spring constant
\(m\): Mass of the oscillating object
